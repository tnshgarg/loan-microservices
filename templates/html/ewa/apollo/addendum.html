NPA CLASSIFICATION

As per the RBI Circular dated 22nd October 2021, Non performing asset (‘NPA’) classification for the lender being a Base Layer NBFC is as follows:

1.    150 days or more overdue will be considered NPA by March 2024

2.    120 days or more overdue will be considered NPA by March 2025

3.    90 days or more overdue will be considered NPA by March 2026

 

For instance:

Delinquency in repayment of any EMI due before March 2024 beyond 150 days from the due date will be treated as a Non-Performing Asset.

For an EMI due date 21-01-2022, if no repayment is received till 20-06-2022, then it will be classified as a Non Performing Asset.
